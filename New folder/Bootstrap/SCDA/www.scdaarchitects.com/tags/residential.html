A true luxury apartment comes with a swimming pool

China

January 17, 2017

Weixin QQ - Designing Together

A villa with pool isn't that surprising - even a hotel might also have its own swimming pool. Most developments these days have typical apartment layouts, with the rare provision of a common swimming pool. 
 
Singapore-based SCDA's Soori High Line residences encapsulate its architecture and interiors sensibilities in a collection of 31 private residences, with 16 apartments having their own private pool. Principal and founder Soo Chan has completed many high-rise pools in Singapore, Bangkok, Kuala Lumpur and other parts of Asia - but a high-rise private swimming pool in Manhattan is a rare feat.

SkyTerrace @ Dawson wins RIBA Award for International Excellence 2016

Singapore

October 27, 2016

Sky Terrace @ Dawson has won the RIBA Award for International Excellence for 2016.

Sky Terrace is an ambitious new exploration of social housing in Singapore which include goals of 50% cheaper construction than typical private developments, natural ventilation and the creation of units where three generations of the same family can live together. 

This project has broken new ground for public housing in Singapore and includes features which when incorporated in future public housing will improve the living environment of the vast majority of Singaporeans.

Ayala Park Central Towers featured in Philippine Tatler, Manila Bulletin and Business Mirror

Manila, Philippines

August 12, 2016

SCDA's design for Ayala Land's Park Central Towers was featured in three major Philippine publications. The high end residential towers will rest on a vast 15,860-square metre parcel adjacent to another Ayala Land Premier project, the Roxas Triangle Towers—completing the Roxas Triangle block.

The building’s unique architectural design includes special four-storey high common spaces called Sky Terraces that will house either a gym, swimming pools, residents’ lounge or spas. Dezeen: SkyTerrace @ Dawson listed as one of 30 buildings in contention for new International Prize for Architecture

International

May 10, 2016

RIBA, London -

SkyTerrace @ Dawson by SCDA, led by Soo K. Chan, has been listed as one of 30 buildings in contention for the new RIBA International Prize for Architecture.

SCDA was commissioned by Singapore's Housing Development Boiard (HDB) in 2008 to explore new possibilities in public housing design. The project has won numerous awards, including a Jury Award at the Architizer A+ Awards 2016, the Excellence Award for the Skyrise Greenery Awards 2015, and Best Residential High Rise Architecture for Singapore and Asia Pacific at the Asia Pacific Property Awards 2014 – 2015.  RIBA Website  Dezeen
