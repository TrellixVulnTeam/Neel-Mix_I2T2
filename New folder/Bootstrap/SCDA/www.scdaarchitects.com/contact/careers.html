Contact/Careers

SCDA Architects employs a diverse group of the most talented professionals in the field. In our offices, you will find experienced architects sharing their expertise with up-and-coming team members as the company culture promotes mentoring.

Here, quality control is a priority and everyone is focused on delivering the best design solutions possible. In-house training sessions keep skills sharp. Sponsored Lunch'n Learns help SCDA staff stay up on the latest products, materials and techniques throughout the industry. Intern Architects are provided study materials and supported in their efforts to become registered.

We make time for fun along the way, too! Events organized by the SCDA Employee Recreation Committee – holiday parties, annual picnics, good-natured competitions, and volunteer opportunities – building camaraderie and team spirit throughout the firm.

While SCDA Architects tackles large projects, we are not a big firm, and that is by design. Just as we do not take on every project that comes our way, we are selective about who we hire. However, we are always looking for strong candidates. Please email a cover letter, resume, and PDF version of your portfolio to

jobs@scdaarchitects.com
