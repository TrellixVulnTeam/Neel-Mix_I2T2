建筑设计/新加坡/Riviere

 

Rivière  |  Singapore

The Rivière sits along a prominent curve of the Singapore River, on a historical site within the warehouse district of Robertson Quay.

The project comprises of two 36-storey residential apartments towers, with a large landscaped garden deck, one 4-storey serviced apartments podium and three conserved warehouses for commercial use along the promenade boundary.

The towers are positioned to have maximum unblocked views northwards towards Orchard Road and southwards towards Marina Bay. At the base of the towers, a raised lushly-landscaped garden level provides residents with privacy while enjoying a sensorial connection with surrounding public spaces at street level along the river promenade. An urban corridor is designed to meander across the site and provides public access from Jiak Kim Street to the Singapore River promenade and Jiak Kim Bridge. The serviced apartment podium continues the undulating ridgeline of the conserved warehouses, preserves the urban edge along the riverside promenade and mediates the scale between the warehouses along the waterfront and the residential towers behind.
