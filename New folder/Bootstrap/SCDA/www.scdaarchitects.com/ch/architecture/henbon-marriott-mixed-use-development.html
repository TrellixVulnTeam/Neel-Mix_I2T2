建筑设计/Chengdu/Henbon

 

HENBON MARRIOTT MIXED-USE DEVELOPMENT  |  Chengdu, China

Nestled within the lush wetland greeneries and across Dong’an Lake, Henbon Mixed-used development takes full advantage of its privileged location, envisioning an iconic garden living lifestyle at its door step. Its prominent position allows for uninterrupted views towards surrounding eco-park as well as Long Quan Mountain. The design solution is an iconic statement that celebrates the seamless weaving between the building architecture and nature. The masterplan is conceived as a distinctive green enclave in which one can experience calm and quietness amidst the hustle and bustle of a dynamic, thriving Chengdu metropolitan city.
 
In term of spatial structure, the hotel tower is placed in the most prominent position with sweeping vistas across the wetland and along its landscape banks of eco-park. The hotel and office tower is a sculptural statement that anchors the corner of the site, offering unparalleled view towards Long Quan mountain. The residential towers complete the architectural composition, providing a backdrop to the overall layered profile along the Dong’an Lake skyline.
