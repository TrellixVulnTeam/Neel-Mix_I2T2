Ayala Park Central Towers featured in Philippine Tatler, Manila Bulletin and Business Mirror

Manila, Philippines

August 12, 2016

SCDA's design for Ayala Land's Park Central Towers was featured in three major Philippine publications. The high end residential towers will rest on a vast 15,860-square metre parcel adjacent to another Ayala Land Premier project, the Roxas Triangle Towers—completing the Roxas Triangle block.

The building’s unique architectural design includes special four-storey high common spaces called Sky Terraces that will house either a gym, swimming pools, residents’ lounge or spas. Anchoring the luxury appointments and central location is an upscale retail podium that will house highly-curated shops and restaurants.  Philippine Tatler  The Manila Bulletin  Business Mirror
