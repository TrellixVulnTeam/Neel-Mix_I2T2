Sanya EDITION named Best in Show at Hospitality Design Awards 2017

New York

June 7, 2017

Hospitality Design (HD) magazine announced project and product winners during the 13th annual HD Awards ceremony in New York at Cipriani 25 Broadway last night. The Sanya EDITION was named Best in Show in projects, while Spokes Large from Foscarini won in products. Sponsors included Durkan, Interiors from Spain, Garden on the Wall, Lexmark + Hospitality, Waterworks, and RH Contract.

 

Nearly 640 project submissions were submitted across 20 categories, including Luxury/Upscale Hotel, Sustainable Project, Lifestyle Hotel, and Casual Restaurant. In the products section of the awards, 330 products were submitted in 11 categories, including Flooring, Casegoods + Furniture, and Bath.

 

A celebrated panel of judges selected this year’s winners for projects and products. For projects: Timothy Griffin, director of North America, Ennismore/the Hoxton; Nicole Hollis, founder, NICOLEHOLLIS; Kemper Hyers, senior vice president of design, Starwood Capital; Helen Jorgensen, vice president of design and procurement, Host Hotels & Resorts; and Alessandro Munge, founder, Studio Munge. For products: Siobhan Barry, partner and executive creator director, dash design; Joan Cardy, founder and president, the Cardy Group; and Gregory Stanford, director of interior design, Marriott International.
