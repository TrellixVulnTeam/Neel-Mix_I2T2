About/Team

ARCHITECTURE DIRECTOR

JIN A. OON  |  Director

Jin Oon is a UK-trained and registered architect. Jin graduated from University of Manchester and obtained his PostGraduate Diploma in Architecture at University College London, Bartlett School of Architecture and received his professional registration in the UK in 1996.

MALCOLM MCCULLOCH  |  Director

Malcolm McCulloch is a Director at SCDA with 20 years of professional working experience. Malcolm graduated from the University of Strathclyde, Glasgow in 1996, and started his professional career in Singapore the same year he received his registration with the Singapore Board of Architects in 2005.

LEE KIT HUNG  |  Director

Lee Kit Hung (Kit) is a registered Architect with Singapore Board of Architects and corporate member of Singapore Institute of Architects. He graduated from National University of Singapore with a Bachelor of Arts (Architecture) and Master of Architecture. Kit started his professional career in 2008 and is currently a Director at SCDA.

INTERIOR DESIGN DIRECTOR

SHEE LENG YAP  |  Director

Shee Leng Yap graduated from the National University of Singapore with a Bachelor of Arts (Architectural Studies) and obtained a Bachelor of Interior Design (Honours) from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology University in 2003. Shee Leng started her professional career in 2004 and is currently a Director at SCDA, specialising in hospitality and residential interiors.
